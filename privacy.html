MathFeedback Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

MathFeedback is a local-first app for recording after-class mathematics feedback.

Data Collection

MathFeedback does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Student names, class names, notes, feedback records, ratings, homework notes, and exported reports are stored locally on the user's device.

Data Storage

The app stores data using Apple's SwiftData framework on the device. The app does not upload app data to any server operated by the developer.

Users can export a JSON backup from the app and choose where to save or share it. Once exported, the user controls that file and is responsible for where it is stored.

Third-Party Services

MathFeedback does not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, or cloud backend services.

Children's Data

The app may be used by teachers or tutors to record student-related notes. This information remains on the user's device unless the user exports or shares it. Users should protect their device, backups, and exported reports appropriately.

Updates
